Privacy Policy

AI File Organizer — Last updated February 10, 2026

Overview

AI File Organizer ("the App") is developed by Arxyn Inc. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data the App accesses and how it is handled.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data. The App does not send any information to Arxyn Inc. servers. We have no analytics, telemetry, tracking, or crash reporting that transmits data to us.

File Access

The App accesses files and folders on your Mac only when you explicitly select them. File contents are processed locally on your device using Apple's Vision framework for OCR and text extraction. Files are never uploaded to our servers.

AI Provider Communication

When you configure an AI provider (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, xAI, or a custom endpoint), the App sends file metadata and extracted text to your chosen provider's API to generate rename suggestions. This communication is:

  • Initiated only by your explicit action (scanning files)
  • Sent directly from your Mac to the provider — not through our servers
  • Governed by the provider's own privacy policy
  • Completely optional — you can use Ollama for 100% local processing

Local AI (Ollama)

When using Ollama, all AI processing happens entirely on your Mac. No data leaves your device.

API Keys

Your API keys are stored securely in the macOS Keychain and are never transmitted to Arxyn Inc. or any third party.

Audit Logs

The App maintains a local audit log of file operations (renames, moves, undos) stored on your Mac in JSONL format. These logs never leave your device.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at support@arxyn.com.
